About Agapeland Daycare

Agapeland Daycare is a place built on the foundation of 'agape' - unconditional love. We believe that every child is a unique gift from God and deserves a loving and supportive environment to grow. Our curriculum is designed to foster spiritual, cognitive, social, and physical development through play-based learning and Christ-centered principles.
